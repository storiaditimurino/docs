# Referrals

Earn USDC by inviting friends to trade on PolyX. Access the referral hub from the main menu by tapping **“Referrals”**.

## Your Referral Hub

The referral screen shows:

- **Your referral code** — tap to copy
- **Invite link** — a shareable Telegram deeplink
- **Network Metrics**
  - Tier 1 Direct — users who signed up with your link
  - Total Reach — all users in your referral network
- **Earnings Dashboard**
  - Claimable USDC — earnings ready to withdraw
  - Total Earned USDC — all-time referral earnings

## Sharing Your Link

Your invite link looks like: `https://t.me/polyxcopy_bot?start=ref_YOURCODE`Two ways to share it:

1. **Copy from the hub** — the code and link are displayed in copyable format
2. **Tap “Copy Link”** — sends a separate message with your invite link for easy forwarding

When someone opens your link in Telegram, they’re directed to PolyX and your referral is tracked automatically.

## Changing Your Referral Code

1. Tap **“Change Ref Code”** on the referral hub
2. Enter a new code (3-20 characters, letters, numbers, and underscores only)
3. If the code is available, it’s updated immediately

If the code is already taken, you’ll be prompted to try a different one.

## Withdrawing Earnings

Referral earnings accumulate in your claimable balance. A minimum withdrawal amount is displayed on the hub (shown with a checkmark if you’ve reached it, or a warning if not).

<Note>
  Referral withdrawals are coming soon. The withdraw button on the referral hub will be enabled in a future update.
</Note>

## How Referral Earnings Work

You earn**50% of the trading fees** generated by users who sign up through your referral link.
