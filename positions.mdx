# Positions

The Positions screen shows all your Polymarket positions with real-time P&L tracking. Access it from the main menu or with `/positions`.

## Open Positions

The main positions view shows:

- **Positions Value** — total USD value of all open positions
- **Total P&L** — combined profit and loss across all positions
- **Open positions count**

Each position entry displays:

- Market title and your outcome (e.g., “Yes” or “No”)
- Current price and P&L percentage
- Entry price vs. current price
- Invested amount vs. current value
- Number of shares held
- Expiration date
- Status badge (Open, Won, or Lost)
- A visual probability bar

### Pagination

Positions are displayed 3 per page. Use **Previous** and **Next** buttons to navigate between pages.

### Empty State

If you don’t have any positions yet, you’ll see a prompt to start trading by exploring markets.

## Position Detail

Tap any position to see its full details. The position detail screen shows all the information from the list view, plus:

- Your cash balance
- Open limit orders for this market (if any)
- Full buy and sell controls

### Market Tab (Default)

The Market tab provides instant trading controls:\
\
**Buy more:**

- Preset amount buttons (e.g., 10,10,50, \$100 from your [**settings**](https://docs.pollyx.trade/settings))
- Custom amount input

**Sell:**

- **Sell Initial** — recover your initial investment (only available when profitable)
- Preset percentage buttons (e.g., 25%, 50%, 75%, 100%)
- Custom percentage input

### Limit Tab

Switch to the Limit tab for limit order controls:

- **Buy Limit** — set a limit buy at a price you specify
- **Limit Sell 100%** — sell entire position at a target price
- **Limit Sell X%** — sell a custom percentage at a target price
- **Multiplier presets** — one-tap sell at a multiple of current price (e.g., “Sell 50% at 2x”)

### Limit Orders on Position

If you have active limit orders for the position’s market, they’re displayed below the position details with:

- Side (Buy/Sell) and value
- Trigger price vs. current price
- Fill progress
- Cancel link for each order

## Closed Positions

Switch to the **Closed** tab from the positions screen to view resolved positions.Closed positions show final outcomes, resolved P&L, and options to generate PNL cards.

## PNL Cards

From any position (open or closed), you can generate a shareable **PNL Card** — an image showing your position details and performance. The card is generated asynchronously and sent as a photo message.

## Redeeming Resolved Positions

When a market resolves and your position has won, you’ll see a **“Claim”** link with the claimable amount. Tap it to redeem your winnings. Lost positions show a **“Close”** link to clear the position from your list.
