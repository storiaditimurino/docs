# Fees

Merlin charges a **0.49% commission** on all trades. This applies to:

- **Market orders** — buy and sell
- **Limit orders** — buy and sell
- **Copy trading** — all automatically copied trades

The fee is calculated on the filled amount and deducted automatically when the order is matched. Unmatched or cancelled orders incur no fee.

<Note>
  This fee is separate from Polymarket’s own market fees, which vary per market.
</Note>
