# Deposit & Withdraw

## Depositing Funds

To deposit funds into your Merlin wallet:

1. Go to **Wallet** \> tap your wallet
2. Find the deposit address for your preferred chain
3. Tap the address to copy it
4. Send tokens from your external wallet to that address

### Supported Deposit Chains

| **Chain** | **Tokens** | **Address Type** |
| :-- | :-- | :-- |
| **Polygon** | USDC, USDC.e | Polymarket proxy (Safe) |
| **Solana** | SOL, USDC, USDT | Solana wallet |
| **Ethereum** | ETH, USDC, USDT | EVM wallet |
| **BASE** | ETH, USDC, USDT | EVM wallet |
| **BNB** | BNB, USDC, USDT | EVM wallet |
| **Bitcoin** | BTC | Bitcoin address |

Each chain section includes a block explorer link so you can verify your deposit.

<Warning>
  Minimum deposit amount is **\$10** worth of tokens. Deposits below this threshold may not be credited.
</Warning>

## Withdrawing Funds

To withdraw funds from your wallet:

### Step 1: Start the Withdrawal

1. Go to **Wallet** \> tap your wallet \> tap **“Withdraw”**
2. Select a destination chain (Ethereum, Polygon, Solana, BASE, or BSC)

### Step 2: Set the Amount

Choose how much to withdraw using any of these options:

- **50%** or **100%** — preset percentage buttons
- **Custom %** — type an integer percentage (1-100)
- **Custom USDC** — type an exact USDC amount

The form shows your current cash balance and the minimum withdrawal amount for the selected chain.

### Step 3: Set the Destination Address

Tap **“Set destination address"** and enter the wallet address where you want to receive funds.

- For EVM chains (Ethereum, Polygon, BASE, BSC): enter an `0x` address
- For Solana: enter a Solana wallet address

### Step 4: Confirm

Once both amount and address are set, the “**Confirm and withdraw**” button appears. Tap it to submit You’ll see a “**Withdrawal Submitted**” confirmation. The withdrawal is processed in the background and the message updates when complete.

<Tip>
  The withdrawal form remembers your entries. You can set the amount and address in any order, and change either one before confirming.
</Tip>

### Minimum Withdrawal Amounts

Each chain has a minimum withdrawal amount. The exact minimums are shown on the withdrawal form when you select a chain. If your amount is below the minimum, you’ll see an error message.

### Cancellation

- Tap **“Cancel”** during any text input step to return to the form without changes
- Tap **“Back”** to return to the chain selection screen
- Navigate away at any time — the withdrawal is only submitted when you tap “Confirm”
